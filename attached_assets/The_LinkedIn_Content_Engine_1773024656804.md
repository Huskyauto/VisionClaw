# SKILL.md — The LinkedIn Content Engine

## Description
Turn your OpenClaw into a LinkedIn content machine. Generate scroll-stopping posts using proven frameworks, manage a weekly content calendar, and build thought leadership that drives inbound leads.

## Prerequisites
- An OpenClaw instance
- A LinkedIn account
- Knowledge of your target audience

## Commands
- "Draft a LinkedIn post about [topic]"
- "Create my content calendar for this week"
- "Generate 5 post ideas for [niche/industry]"
- "Rewrite this post to be more engaging: [paste post]"
- "Write a hook for [topic]"
- "Turn this idea into a LinkedIn carousel outline: [idea]"
- "Analyze this post and tell me why it worked/didn't work"
- "Generate a week of content around [theme]"

## Content Frameworks

### Framework 1: Hook → Story → Lesson
```
[Provocative opening line — pattern interrupt, surprising stat, or bold claim]

[blank line — forces the "see more" click]

[2-3 sentences of context or personal story]

[The insight, lesson, or tactical takeaway — this is the value]

[Call to action: question, invitation to comment, or soft CTA]
```

### Framework 2: The Listicle
```
[X] things I learned about [topic] after [experience]:

1. [Point] — [one-line explanation]
2. [Point] — [one-line explanation]
3. [Point] — [one-line explanation]
...

Which one resonates most? 👇
```

### Framework 3: Contrarian Take
```
Unpopular opinion: [statement that challenges conventional wisdom]

Here's why:

[3-4 sentences of reasoning with evidence or personal experience]

[Acknowledge the other side briefly]

[Restate your position with confidence]

Agree or disagree? 👇
```

### Framework 4: Before/After Transformation
```
[Time period] ago, [subject] was struggling with [specific problem].

[Paint the pain — make it relatable]

Today: [impressive, specific result]

Here's what changed (it wasn't what you'd expect):

1. [Change #1 — the non-obvious insight]
2. [Change #2 — the practical shift]
3. [Change #3 — the mindset shift]

The biggest takeaway: [one-line lesson]

[CTA]
```

### Framework 5: The How-To
```
How to [achieve desirable outcome] in [timeframe]:

Step 1: [Action] → [Why it matters]
Step 2: [Action] → [Why it matters]
Step 3: [Action] → [Why it matters]

Most people skip Step [X]. That's why they fail.

[Expand on the most important step]

Save this for later 🔖
```

### Framework 6: Personal Story
```
[Vulnerable or surprising opening — "I got fired." / "I almost quit." / "The worst advice I ever followed:"]

[Set the scene — when, where, what happened]

[The turning point — what changed]

[What you learned — the universal lesson]

[How others can apply this]
```

### Framework 7: The Poll/Question
```
Quick question for [your audience]:

[Specific, relevant question that people have opinions about]

I'll go first: [Your answer in 1-2 sentences]

Drop yours below 👇
```

## Hook Formulas (Scroll-Stoppers)

1. **The Bold Claim:** "Most [professionals] are doing [thing] completely wrong."
2. **The Number:** "[X]% of [group] fail at [thing]. Here's why:"
3. **The Confession:** "I used to [common mistake]. Here's what I learned:"
4. **The Question:** "What would you do if [scenario]?"
5. **The Contradiction:** "[Common belief] is a lie. Here's the truth:"
6. **The Timestamp:** "[Time] ago I [situation]. Today I [result]."
7. **The Hot Take:** "Stop [popular advice]. It's ruining your [outcome]."
8. **The List Tease:** "The 3 things that changed my [area] forever:"
9. **The Story Open:** "I'll never forget the day [vivid moment]."
10. **The Direct Address:** "If you're a [role] struggling with [problem], read this:"

## Content Calendar Template

### Weekly Schedule
- **Monday:** Industry insight or trend analysis (Thought Leadership)
- **Tuesday:** How-to or tactical tip (Value Post)
- **Wednesday:** Case study or results story (Social Proof)
- **Thursday:** Personal story or lesson learned (Authenticity)
- **Friday:** Question, poll, or engagement post (Community)

### Content Pillars
Define 3-5 topics you'll consistently post about:
1. **Your Core Expertise** — what you know better than most
2. **Industry Insights** — trends, observations, predictions
3. **Results & Proof** — case studies, metrics, wins
4. **Lessons & Stories** — personal experiences, failures, growth
5. **Audience Engagement** — questions, debates, community building

## Post Optimization Checklist
- [ ] First line is a scroll-stopper (would YOU stop scrolling?)
- [ ] Second line is blank (forces "see more" click on mobile)
- [ ] Post is scannable (short paragraphs, line breaks, bullets)
- [ ] Contains one clear insight or takeaway
- [ ] Ends with engagement driver (question, CTA, invitation)
- [ ] No external links in the post (LinkedIn suppresses reach — put links in comments)
- [ ] Relevant hashtags (3-5 max, mix of broad and niche)
- [ ] Tagged relevant people only if genuinely relevant
- [ ] Proofread for typos and tone

## Batch Content Creation Prompt
```
Based on my content pillars [list them] and target audience [describe them], generate:
1. Five LinkedIn post drafts for this week following my content calendar
2. Each post should use a different framework
3. Each post should have a strong hook from the hook formulas
4. Include suggested hashtags for each post
5. Flag which post is likely to perform best and why
```

## Engagement Strategy
- Reply to every comment on your posts within 2 hours
- Comment on 5-10 posts from your target audience daily
- Add value in comments (not just "Great post!")
- Share and comment on content from people you want to connect with
- Post consistently at the same times (test and find your optimal times)

## Guardrails
- Never post content that misrepresents the user's expertise
- Always maintain the user's authentic voice — adapt to their style
- Flag any content that could be controversial and confirm before posting
- Don't use engagement bait tactics (like/comment farming)
- Quality over quantity — one great post beats five mediocre ones
